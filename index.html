How does it work?

Through Bundlee’s unique circular model, you can rent premium Clarks Kids shoes (and lots of brilliant clothing brands, too) for a monthly subscription fee – and swap them hassle-free for fresh pairs whenever your little ones are ready for a different size.

So, you can enjoy updating their mini wardrobes with seasonal shoes that always fit them perfectly – while saving money, saving space and reducing waste. Win-win!

As Easy As 1,2,3

1. Rent

Pick your favourite Clarks Kids shoes to rent along with pieces from other brands in your little one’s size. They’ll be delivered to your door, sealed with Bundlee’s Cleanliness Guarantee.

2. Wear

Keep the shoes and clothes for as long as they fit. Your Bundlee Plan includes Rental Insurance to cover life’s messy moments, with no charges for stains or wear and tear. Walks in the wild, stomping around the playground – they can enjoy their shoes to the max!

3. Swap

Little one growing at a rate of knots? You can swap outgrown shoes and clothes for the next size whenever you need to, with free returns, reusable packaging and a collect-from-home service. Bundlee then professionally cleans and sanitises the pieces to get them ready for the next renting family to love.
